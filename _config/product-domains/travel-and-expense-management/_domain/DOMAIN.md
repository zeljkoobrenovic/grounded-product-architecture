# Travel and Expense Management

This domain models the core business Rydoo operates in: business travel and expense management for mid-market and enterprise companies.

Why this domain:

- Rydoo's core public positioning centers on expense management, travel expense flows, mileage, per diem, approvals, policy, card reconciliation, and finance integrations.
- Rydoo also extends into cards, travel booking integrations, analytics, smart audit, and AP-adjacent workflows, which fit a broader business spend operating model.
- A travel-and-expense frame fits the repository better than a narrow AP-only model because the employee traveler, approver, and finance operations loops are the primary value engine.
