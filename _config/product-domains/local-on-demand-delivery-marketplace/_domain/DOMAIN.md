# Local On-Demand Delivery Marketplace

This domain models the local delivery marketplace operated by companies such as Delivery Hero, Uber Eats, DoorDash, Grab, talabat, Deliveroo, and Just Eat Takeaway.com.

The domain boundary includes restaurant delivery, grocery and convenience delivery, courier logistics, merchant growth tooling, marketplace ads, subscriptions, payments, support, safety, and marketplace intelligence. It excludes ride hailing, freight, travel, and general e-commerce fulfillment unless those services materially support local on-demand delivery.

The domain was selected because it is the clearest overlap between Delivery Hero and Uber Eats: both operate food delivery marketplaces, both are expanding toward everyday local commerce, and both monetize through consumer fees, merchant commissions, delivery economics, advertising, subscriptions, and adjacent quick-commerce use cases.
