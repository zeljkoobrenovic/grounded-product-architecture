# Industrial Water Intelligence

This domain models one of Ecolab's clearest operating cores: connected industrial water treatment, monitoring, optimization, compliance, and reuse across water-intensive sites.

Why this domain:

- Ecolab's `Global Water` segment is the company's largest reported segment and, as of the 2025 reporting alignment, includes Light & Heavy, Food & Beverage, and Paper.
- Ecolab publicly positions `3D TRASAR`, `ECOLAB3D`, and `Water Quality IQ` around real-time monitoring, alarming, connected chemistry, wastewater visibility, enterprise benchmarking, and measurable water savings.
- The model fits the repository well because it connects customer outcomes, site operations, digital monitoring, service workflows, and reusable product bricks without collapsing into a narrow point solution.

Domain hypothesis:

1. The core buying center is operational, not purely IT-led: utilities, plant operations, water programs, environment, and sustainability leaders are the main economic decision-makers.
2. The product system is hybrid by design: sensors, chemistry, software, analytics, and field experts work as one commercial and operational system.
3. Expansion depends on proving value site by site, then standardizing that value across an enterprise fleet through digital visibility and repeatable treatment programs.
4. Compliance, uptime, and water savings matter together; the winning offer is not just lower water use, but stable production with fewer excursions and faster operator response.
5. Water reuse, wastewater optimization, and enterprise benchmarking are strong expansion paths once basic monitoring and treatment control are trusted.
