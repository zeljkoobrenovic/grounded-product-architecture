# Cloud Data and AI Platform

This domain models the public-cloud subdomain where providers help enterprises build, govern, operate, and monetize data and AI workloads. It is intentionally narrower than the full Public Cloud Services domain: it focuses on cloud data platforms, analytics, AI infrastructure, foundation model access, ML operations, governance, and AI application delivery.

Why this domain:

- Public cloud growth is increasingly pulled by data gravity, AI infrastructure demand, managed analytics, and enterprise AI adoption.
- The same customer journey spans data ingestion, lakehouse or warehouse storage, model development, governed serving, observability, and cost control.
- The competitive landscape combines hyperscalers such as AWS, Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud, Alibaba Cloud, and Oracle Cloud with specialist data platforms such as Snowflake and Databricks.

Primary modeled customer loops:

- Enterprise data and AI leaders consolidating governed data platforms and AI workloads
- AI application builders moving from prototypes to production model and agent experiences
- Platform, security, and FinOps teams operating data and AI services with reliability, compliance, and unit-cost discipline
