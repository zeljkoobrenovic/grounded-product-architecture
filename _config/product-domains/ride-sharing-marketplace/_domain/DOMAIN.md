# Ride Sharing Marketplace

This domain models a two-sided ride sharing marketplace similar to Uber's core Mobility business.

Why this domain:

- `uber.com` is most strongly identified with on-demand urban ride sharing, not only broader local commerce.
- The business depends on a live marketplace balancing rider demand, driver supply, pricing, dispatch, safety, and city-specific operating rules.
- Uber's current public product surfaces also show adjacent but still ride-centric extensions such as `Reserve`, `UberX Share`, teen accounts, airport products, shuttles, and Uber for Business ground transportation.

Primary reference themes:

- Consumer rider mobility
- Driver and fleet supply
- Marketplace pricing and dispatch
- Safety and trust controls
- Business travel and travel-program administration
