# Payments and Revenue Infrastructure

This domain models Stripe as programmable financial infrastructure for internet businesses rather than as a narrow payment gateway.

Why this domain:

- Stripe's core public surface spans payments, subscriptions, invoicing, tax, fraud, identity, and platform payouts.
- The same merchant often adopts Stripe across checkout conversion, recurring monetization, finance automation, and platform money movement.
- The compounding loops are operational, not only transactional: payment success drives retention, billing drives revenue automation, Connect drives ecosystem growth, and Radar / Identity / ledgering protect the whole system.

Primary modeled customer loops:

- Merchants maximizing checkout conversion and operational reliability
- SaaS and recurring-revenue operators reducing involuntary churn and month-end manual work
- Platforms and marketplaces onboarding counterparties and moving funds safely at scale
